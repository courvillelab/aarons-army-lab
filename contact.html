Contact for Prospective Lab Members


Thank you for your interest in our lab! We are always looking for strong Ph.D students, postdocs and undergraduate students to join our lab. However, due to the volume of email, it is challenging to answer individual requests. Please refer to the follow guidelines for applying and joining our lab.

Prospective PhD Students

We are looking for strong Ph.D. students to join our group! We ask that you do not contact us directly with regard to PhD admissions until after you are admitted, as we will not be able to reply to emails from individual applicants. Please apply to Mila - Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute and at the Université de Montréal to work with us, and list Aaron Courville as a faculty of interest!

Prospective Postdocs

We are looking for postdoc applicants interested in multi agent, reinforcement learning and deep learning. Interested candidates please email Aaron Courville with a CV.

Prospective Undergraduate or MS Students

We work with undergraduate and masters students only at University of Montreal. Unfortunately we do not host undergraduate interns or visitors from other institutions at this time. For prospective undergraduate and masters students, please apply here and and list Aaron Courville as a faculty of interest!
